A short portfolio from one of those rare cloudless weekends at Mount Rainier. The first frame is from the Sunrise area on the east side; the second is the obligatory Tipsoo Lake reflection (worth the predawn alarm); the third is mid-summer wildflower season on the Skyline Trail; the fourth is the Nisqually glacier seen from Glacier Vista, which has retreated visibly in just a decade.
